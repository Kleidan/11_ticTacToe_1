Tic-Tac-Toe

Welcome!

This is the good old classical and beloved tic-tac-toe game, the rules to this game are very simple:

First you need to find a frind to play with. Next you need to decide who goes first and plays as X. On the grid to the right, X places his first mark on one of the cells on the grid. Next goes O and he places his mark on an empty cell.

The Goal of the game is to create a set of three X's or O's. the combination can be horizontal, vertical or diagonal. First to create a set is the winner!
